Flik isn't just a game; it's a test of steady hands and tactical brilliance. Born from the tradition of tabletop skirmishes, every board is hand-carved to ensure a unique friction and speed. Whether you're defending your castle or sieging your opponent's ranks, every flick counts.

The Golden Rules

  • The Strike: Use only one finger to propel your piece.
  • Collision: If you knock an enemy piece off the board, it is removed from play.
  • The Boundary: Any piece that crosses the carved perimeter is considered "In Exile."
  • Victory: Be the last House standing on the timber.
